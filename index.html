I’m a Ph.D candidate at Hasso Plattner Institute, with Prof. Patrick Baudisch. My research fields are mainly human-computer interaction (HCI) and personal fabrication. In particular, I’m interested in repurposing things that we already have or use in daily life in order to create functional objects and physical experience.
