Musikinstrumenten-Museum Berlin

2026-01-04

We visited the Musikinstrumenten-Museum Berlin.

The pipe organ is a modern design and produces a lovely sound. The Cafe in the underground also has a nice atmosphere.

For some reason, it reminded me of the movie “The Shining”, but I don’t know why. Maybe the combination of white and yellow lighting.
